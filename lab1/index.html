All about flowers and plants

Flowers

A flower, also known as a bloom or blossom, is the reproductive structure found in flowering plants(plants of the division Angiospermae). Flowers consist of a combination of vegetative organs sepals that enclose and protect the developing flower.

Plants

Plants are the eukaryotes that form the kingdom Plantae; they are predominantly photosynthetic. This means that they obtain their energy from sunlight, using chloroplasts derived from endosymbiosis with cyanobacteria to produce sugars from carbon dioxide.
